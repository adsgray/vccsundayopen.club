Playoff Rules  Winners of game in RED
1) The higher seeded team gets the hammer and choice of stones.
2) During the regular season spares can play any position but skip (can call the game though). In the playoffs spares can play no higher than second position. Spares are to play lead position fist and the then second in the event that two spares are needed.
3) You must have 2 members of your team playing to register a win.
4) In the event of a tie we use club bonspiel rules - draw to the button (only throwing team may sweep)
*) For the # 1/2 Final Game, if both teams agree to play on a different sheet, they have the right to switch ice with any game as long as they inform that sheet before the teams enter the ice shed
